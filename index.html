Who am I?

I'm passionate about writing beautiful code that creates beautiful things.

Hello there. I'm a senior studying computer science at SD Mines in Rapid City, South Dakota. I'm originally from Washington State and will be moving back to the Pacific North-Left in May for a full-time position with Microsoft after graduation. Right now I'm focused on my senior design project with CHR Solutions, a company I've been with for almost two years.

I have a wide variety of interests - I love full-stack development, data science and design. My favorite kinds of projects are ones where I get my hands on each part of the application. I'm also a numbers nerd and like looking at data.

When I'm not behind a computer, I love to go outside. Hiking, hammocking, walking around town and participating in the community are all a part of my regular routine. I'm an amateur astronomer, so I keep my eyes on the sky with my telescope and star maps.

  • 2011

    Started at University of Washington

    After high school I knew I wanted to go into a STEM field, but was unsure about which major to pick. While at UW, I worked in a bioengineering lab and completed a 3-month oceanography research apprenticeship in the San Juan Islands.

  • January 2014

    Started at SD Mines

    Failing to find direction at UW, I transferred to SD Mines and took an introductory programming class - and something clicked.

  • Summer 2014 and 2015

    Department of Energy Internships

    For two consecutive summers I interned with the Department of Energy Office of River Protection in Richland, Washington. I did work on their SharePoint websites and helped design informational sheets for the public.

  • Autumn 2015

    Tech Fellow at SD Mines

    I helped with hardware and software support for our school-issued tablet PCs.

  • November 2015

    Started at CHR Solutions

    This internship involved creating billing software for telecommunications companies, and got experience with business logic, GUI programming and learned what being a software developer was like .

  • Summer 2017

    On the Fargo campus, I worked on the Dynamics 365 for Talent team on a Company News and Events feature using extensible controls.

  • August 2017

    CHR Solutions senior design

    I returned to CHR Solutions this year to participate in a industry-sponsored senior design project to poll legacy telecom switches for usage data.

  • May 2018

    Graduation and start work at Microsoft

    May 2018 will see me graduating with a degree in computer science and minor in math. I'll also be starting work on the OneDrive Service team at Microsoft.
